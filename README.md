# Stake-meteor

A bright, single-page HTML5 meteor betting game inspired by your meteor art. Adjust the bet, watch the cursor sweep, and launch the meteor to collect multipliers, shields, or avoid the water.

## Run locally

1. Clone the repo.
2. Open `index.html` in your browser.

## How to play

- Set a bet amount and press **Launch** when the cursor hits the desired power zone.
- **Missiles** halve your bet and shrink the meteor.
- **Multiplier zone** boosts your bet.
- **Shield zone** grants extra shields.
- **Water zone** ends the round as a loss.

## Controls

- Launch with the button on the right panel.
- Adjust bet using the input field.

## Notes

All visuals are drawn on the canvas, so the game runs with no build step.
